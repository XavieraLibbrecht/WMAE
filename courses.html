WFA - Wilderness First Aid

An introduction to general medical concepts and basic life support skills, targeted to the outdoor enthusiast on day trips or short adventures.

WAFA - Wilderness Advanced First Aid

Wilderness Advanced First Aid is comprehensive medical training designed for remote professionals or wilderness leaders who venture into remote and challenging environments.

Bridge (WAFA to WFR)

The WAFA to WFR Bridge course upgrades the Wilderness Advanced First Aid to a Wilderness First Responder (WFR) in 4 days.

WFR - Wilderness First Responder

The course provides essential training in wilderness medicine, leadership and critical thinking for professionals and leaders working and travelling in outdoor, low-resource and remote environments. During the course, students learn the essential principles and skills required to assess and manage medical problems in isolated and extreme environments for days and weeks if necessary.

WFR Recertification

The WFR Recertification course is designed to recertify and refresh current Wilderness First Responders.
